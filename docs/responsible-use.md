# Responsible Use

Universal Downloader is a personal tool for downloading **content you have the right to download**.

## Your Responsibilities

- **Respect copyright.** Do not download or redistribute copyrighted material without permission.
- **Respect Terms of Service.** Each supported site has its own ToS:
  - YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms
  - X (Twitter): https://twitter.com/tos
  - Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/terms.php
  - Reddit: https://www.redditinc.com/policies/user-agreement
- **Respect rate limits.** Excessive requests can result in your IP being blocked by the upstream site. The default concurrency limit is `3`.
- **Don't publish redistributions.** Personal, offline use only.

## What This Tool Does Not Do

- Bypass DRM, paywalls, or age gates that require authentication you don't possess.
- Download private content you cannot otherwise access.
- Anonymise your traffic — your IP is visible to upstream sites.
